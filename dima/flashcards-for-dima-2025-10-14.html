What are three congnitive processes, or mental activities, involved in programming?
Short Term Memory (STM), Long Term Memory (LTM), and Working Memory.
Define Long Term Memory (LTM)
The place where all your memories, or knowledge, are stored for a long period of time.
Define Short Term Memory (STM)
The place where you temprarily store information you are gathering. Your STM is used to briefly hold incoming informaion. The STM has a limited size. When your brain has solved the problme at hand your STM is emptied.
Define Working Memory
The place where your thinking happens. Working memory where the information from STM and LTM is processed while you think about it. This is where new thoughts, ideas, and solutions are formed.
What is a variable
A variable is a "named" container or storage for data.
What does the keyword "let" do?
It tells or declares JavaScript that you are creating a variable.
What is the name of the following operator?
=
The assignment operator. It is used to assign, or put, data into a variable.
What characters can be used for a variable's name?
a-z, A-Z, 0-9, $, _
What is camel case?
The practice of capitalizing each word in a variable name except for the first word.
What keyword can you use to see the "type" of a variable?
typeof
